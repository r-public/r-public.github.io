ANNIVERSARY

A General Room Review Meeting for R Public chatroom

On the 5th of March, at UTC 18:00, We're going to have a fun party an event at the R Public Chatroom and will last for about 15 minutes.

Be there or Be Square

Background

It has been an year after the room was un-frozen and re-modeled. This small meeting is to mark first anniversary of the room and to look back at the past year.

Agenda

  1. Did the room face any issues in the past year?
  2. Do we need a change in the room owners (any addition or reduction)?

Feel free to ping any of the room regulars to add your topic for discussion.

The event concluded successfully with 4 participants. Do go through the meeting transcript below. The conversation is bookmarked for future reference.

The outcome of the room meeting can be enlisted as follows,

  1. Did the room face any issues in the past year?

    – The answer was a resounding NO. There was no untoward incident which the room regulars could recall.

  2. Do we need a change in the room owners (any addition or reduction)?

    – It was concluded that there was no solid reason to un-RO any room owner. Furthermore, it was decided to elevate Matthew, to the position of a room owner.
